The Six Core Modules

1. Intelligent CRM

A conversational, next-gen CRM that automatically logs interactions, scores leads, and suggests optimal next actions using Natural Language Processing.

  • • Conversation-driven pipelines (Slack, Email, WhatsApp)
  • • Predictive lead scoring and automated routing
  • • Zero manual data entry required

2. AI Phone Caller

Automated outbound and inbound calling with ultra-low latency, human-like voices for sales qualification, appointment setting, and support.

  • • Contextual, human-like speech
  • • 24/7 sales qualification
  • • Call outcome logging directly to CRM

3. AI Lead Scraper

Targeted data extraction from high-value public sources and specialized platforms, providing verified, deduplicated, and normalized leads daily.

  • • Custom rule-based scraping for specific industries
  • • Real-time data validation
  • • Direct API integration with your existing CRM/DMS

4. AI Workflows

Automation pipelines that chain together multiple tasks: processing documents, routing support tickets, generating reports, and automating back-office tasks.

  • • LLM-powered document summarization
  • • Integration with ERP and accounting software

24/7 contextual virtual agents for customer support, onboarding, and scheduling, capable of answering complex queries based on your knowledge base.

  • • Multi-channel deployment (web, social, app)
  • • Seamless handoff to human agents
  • • High-accuracy knowledge retrieval

6. Custom AI Solutions

Bespoke model training and deployment for domain-specific problems: prediction, anomaly detection, and specialized data classification.
